Why SmartFridge AI?

The antidote to food waste.

Every year, a third of all food produced is wasted. Most of it starts in the fridge. SmartFridge AI gives you eyes inside yours — so nothing spoils unnoticed.

📸

ESP32-CAM scanning

A low-cost camera module captures clear images of your produce. No cloud required — everything runs on-device.

🧠

Vision AI model

A lightweight machine learning model classifies freshness from visual cues — color, texture, surface defects.

⚡

Instant results

Analysis completes in under 2 seconds. The dashboard updates in real time, no manual refresh needed.

📊

Confidence scores

Every result comes with a confidence percentage, so you understand exactly how certain the model is.

🔔

Rotten alerts

The moment something goes bad, the dashboard flags it with a clear alert — no guessing, no squinting.

🌐

Web dashboard

Access the live freshness report from any browser on your network. Clean, readable, always up to date.
